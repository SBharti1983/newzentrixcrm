# 🚀 ZentrixCRM — Getting Started Guide

Welcome to ZentrixCRM! This manual will help you and your team manage real estate leads, projects, and site visits like a pro.

---

## 1. Accessing Your Dashboard
Each company has its own unique URL (Tenant Subdomain).
*   **Your URL**: `https://mayainfratech.zentrixcrm.com`
*   **Login**: Enter your official email and the password provided by your company administrator.

---

## 2. Managing Leads (The Heart of CRM)
Located at: **Leads** & **Pipeline**

Everything starts with a Lead.
- **Add Leads**: Click "Add Lead" to manually enter walk-ins or phone enquiries. 
- **Lead Score**: The AI (Gemini) automatically scores incoming leads (0-100) based on their budget, property interest, and timeline. High scores mean they are ready to buy!
- **Kanban Pipeline**: Use the "Pipeline" view to drag-and-drop leads through stages: 
  *New* → *Contacted* → *Qualified* → *Site Visit* → *Negotiation* → *Won/Lost*.

---

## 3. Real Estate Projects & Inventory
Located at: **Projects** & **Inventory**

Manage your physical real estate developments.
- **Project Details**: Add your different sites (e.g., "Maya Heights").
- **Inventory Tracking**: Assign units (Flats/Villas) and track their real-time availability. 
- **Pricing**: Set price ranges for different unit types (2BHK, 3BHK, etc.).

---

## 4. Sales Lifecycle: Visits & Bookings
Located at: **Site Visits** & **Bookings**

Convert interest into revenue.
- **Scheduling**: Schedule Site Visits for leads. You can even assign an agent's car for transport.
- **Bookings**: When a lead pays the token, create a "Booking". This automatically:
  1. Marks the unit as Sold.
  2. Generates a Payment Plan.
  3. Creates an entry in the **Payment Tracker**.

---

## 5. Payment Tracker & Installments
Located at: **Payment Tracker**

Never miss an installment.
- **Milestone Billing**: Automatically creates future payment dates based on construction progress (e.g., Plinth Level, 4th Floor Slab).
- **Overdue Alerts**: Highlights payments that are late so your team can follow up immediately.
- **Receipts**: Mark installments as "Paid" and record receipt numbers.

---

## 6. Custom Reporting Engine
Located at: **Reports**

ZentrixCRM includes a high-fidelity intelligence hub for institutional analysis.
- **Report Templates**: Choose from curated templates like Lead Velocity, Conversion Attribution, and Executive Sales Summaries.
- **Dynamic Filters**: Slice your data by Project, Lead Source, assigned Agent, or specific date ranges.
- **Visual Analytics**: View live data previews with interactive charts showing lead progression speeds and revenue absorption.
- **Export Center**: Download any report in high-fidelity formats (CSV, PDF, or JSON) for external presentations or accounting.

---

## 7. Billing & Subscription Management
Located at: **Admin > Billing**

Manage your SaaS lifecycle directly from the platform.
- **Plan Overview**: View your current plan (Starter/Pro/Enterprise), usage limits (Active Leads, Projects), and renewal date.
- **Upgrade/Downgrade**: Transition between plans as your real estate firm scales.
- **Payment History**: Access and download GST-compliant invoices for your monthly or annual subscription fees.

---

## 8. Integration Hub (Tech Setup)
Located at: **Integrations** & **Automations**

Connect ZentrixCRM to the outside world.
- **Meta Ads**: Sync your Facebook/Instagram Lead Forms directly.
- **WhatsApp API**: Send automated brochures and follow-up greetings.
- **Google Gemini AI**: Power your "Magic Wand" content generator to draft emails with one click.
- **Magic Distribution**: Set "Lead Round-Robin" rules to automatically assign leads to agents who are currently online.

---

Need help? Click the **"Zapier Assistant"** bubble in the bottom corner or contact your account manager at `support@zentrixcrm.com`.
